Deployment Context: This platform is designed for pilot deployment with local Police Departments and hospitality partners. Officers can monitor crowd conditions in real time, identify high-density venues requiring patrol attention, investigate specific incidents by venue and time window, and anticipate high-activity periods using the predictive forecast module.

Data: 1,200 check-in records across 10 venues in 6 patrol zones — Nightclub, Bar/Lounge, Cafe, Restaurant, Gym, Entertainment, Food Court, Mall, Coworking, and Park. Incident flags are automatically assigned based on crowd density thresholds: CRITICAL ≥85 · HIGH ≥70 · ELEVATED ≥50 · NORMAL <50
